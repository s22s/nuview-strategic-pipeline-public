üìä Pipeline Opportunity Scoring System

The NUVIEW Strategic Pipeline uses a comprehensive scoring algorithm to prioritize opportunities based on multiple factors. Understanding this system helps you identify the highest-value prospects for immediate action.

Scoring Formula

Overall Score = (Value Score √ó 50%) + (Strategic Fit √ó 20%) + (Timing Score √ó 20%) + (Probability √ó 10%)

üí∞ Value Score (50%)

Calculation: Normalized contract value in USD
Formula: min(valueUSD / 1M √ó 0.5, 50)
Impact: Larger opportunities receive higher scores, capped at 50 points

üéØ Strategic Fit (20%)

Factors: LiDAR capability match, space-based DaaS alignment, NUVIEW core competencies
Formula: keywordMatches √ó 4
Impact: Opportunities aligned with NUVIEW strengths score higher

‚è∞ Timing Score (20%)

Factors: Days until deadline, submission window
Urgency Levels: Urgent (<30 days), Near (30-90 days), Future (>90 days)
Impact: Imminent deadlines boost priority, balancing preparation time needs

üìà Probability (10%)

Factors: Data accessibility, agency relationship, past win rate
Formula: 15pts (open) or 5pts (restricted) + confidence √ó 20
Impact: Higher probability of winning increases score

üöÄ Priority Thresholds

  • 90-100: CRITICAL - Immediate action required, executive review
  • 75-89: HIGH - Priority pursuit, assign resources
  • 60-74: MEDIUM - Monitor closely, prepare materials
  • Below 60: LOW - Track for future, no immediate action

Note: Scores are recalculated daily based on updated data from scrapers and QC validation. The system ensures only quality-validated opportunities (QC pass rate >95%) are included in scoring.
